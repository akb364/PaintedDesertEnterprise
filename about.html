About Us

Painted Desert Enterprise specializes in concrete construction, offering a comprehensive range of services. Including, curb and gutter, sidewalks, driveways, valley gutters, drainage spillways, handicap ramps, and demolition. Our highly trained staff and extensive array of specialized equipment ensure that we deliver professional and efficient results in every project.

Owner & Leading manager - Jarett Patton began his career in the construction industry in 1991. With years of experience under his belt, in 2002, he founded Painted Desert Enterprise. As the leading manager, Jarett oversees all projects, estimations, and manages all business relationships.
